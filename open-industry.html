Graph Anatomy

What's inside each industry knowledge graph.

Every graph follows a consistent schema so they're interoperable, comparable, and machine-readable.

◈

Revenue Streams

Segment-level revenue breakdown, growth rates, margin profiles, and cross-segment dependencies.

⬡

Product Topology

Products, services, and platforms mapped as interconnected nodes with dependency and substitution edges.

⦿

Competitive Dynamics

Head-to-head positioning, market share overlaps, moat analysis, and threat vectors between companies.

⭐

AI Strategy

Model investments, compute infrastructure, AI product roadmaps, and partnership/acquisition graphs.

◎

Supply Chain

Key suppliers, manufacturing dependencies, geographic concentration risks, and logistics networks.

✶

Regulatory Landscape

Antitrust actions, privacy regulations, market-specific compliance requirements, and political exposure.

Why Open Source

Intelligence compounds when it's shared.

🌐

Democratize Access

Hedge funds pay millions for industry intelligence. Students, researchers, and small teams deserve the same structured insights — for free.

🔗

Network Effects

Every contributor adds nodes and edges. The graph gets smarter. Cross-company relationships emerge that no single analyst could map alone.

🔬

Verifiable Reasoning

Every claim traces to a source. Every relationship is explicit. No black-box rankings. No hidden methodology. Full transparency.
