The Problem

Healthcare has the data. It doesn't have the structure.

→30% of the world's data is healthcare. Almost none of it is structured for AI reasoning. Trapped in clinical notes, scanned PDFs, fax machines, and siloed EHRs.
→LLMs hallucinate drug interactions. RAG retrieves the wrong protocol. There's no relationship layer between diagnoses, medications, and outcomes.
→Interoperability is mandated. Intelligence isn't. FHIR moves data. It doesn't structure meaning. Structured pipes without structured intelligence just move unstructured data faster.
→$1.4B spent on healthcare AI in 2025. Nearly tripled year-over-year. Most of it wasted because AI pilots lack the structured domain context to operationalize.
